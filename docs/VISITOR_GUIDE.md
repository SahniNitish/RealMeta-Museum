## Frequently Asked Questions

### Does this work offline?
No, you need internet connection to scan artworks. You can browse previously viewed items.

### Why didn't it recognize the artwork?
Try moving closer, improving lighting, or checking the artwork is in this museum's collection.

### Can I save artworks?
Use your phone's screenshot feature or bookmark the artwork page.

### Is this free?
Yes! Museum AI is free for all museum visitors.

### What data is collected?
Only your photos are processed temporarily for matching. No personal data is stored.

---

## Troubleshooting

### Camera Won't Open
- Allow camera permissions in browser
- Try refreshing the page
- Use a different browser (Chrome recommended)

### Artwork Not Recognized
- Move closer to the artwork
- Ensure good lighting
- Try capturing from a different angle
- The artwork may not be in the database yet

### Audio Not Playing
- Check your phone's volume
- Try headphones
- Refresh the page

### Page Won't Load
- Check internet connection
- Scan the QR code again
- Ask museum staff for help
